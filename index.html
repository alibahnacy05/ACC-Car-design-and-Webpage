Introducing the 911 GT3 R


The new Porsche 911 GT3 R is a powerhouse created for motorsports that promises to increase your adrenaline levels. This high-performance vehicle produces about 416 kW (565 HP) and has sophisticated aerodynamics and improved balance, making it faster than before. (Porsche Ref.)


In addition, with wider axles and body panels to improve airflow, the 911 GT3 R is designed for race enthusiasts who want to exceed their limits. Its eye-catching appearance, sponsored by Google, makes it stand out on the track. So prepare to embrace challenges and enjoy the thrill of driving the Porsche 911 GT3 R! (Porsche Ref.)

911 (991) GT3 • 2017-2019


Major improvements were made to the Porsche 911 GT3 in the 991 generation, such as a 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine that produced 500 PS, similar to the 911 GT3 Cup racecar. To handle the extra power, the vehicle also had a new chassis with lightweight construction and rear-wheel steering. (Porsche Ref.)


Moreover, a sizable carbon fiber rear wing was added for improved aerodynamics and a decidedly athletic appearance. Its interior remained simple, with a steering wheel reminiscent of the 918 Spyder. (Porsche Ref.)
