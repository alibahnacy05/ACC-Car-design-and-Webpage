Maserati X Pokemon


I first made this Maserati MC GT4, however the car design was not working on the game, which is why I switched to the Porsche 911 GT3 R. However, I would still like to showcase the first car I have ever designed.


I took inspiration from Pokemon and made them my main sponsor for the car. I applied this by fading the colors of Pokemon and taking Pikachu's ears and tail, putting them on the car.


In addition, I decided to add blue lines in the front of the car to resemble lightening bolts, linking it to Pikachu's abilities and the cars speed.
