Why Google?


Choosing Google as my sponsor for the Porsche 911 GT3 R design ideally combines high-performance racing and cutting-edge technology. Google's reputation for innovation compliments Porsche's engineering skills, establishing my concept as both fast and forward-thinking. (Sponsor Ref.)


This partnership would also increase the car's visibility, using Google's global influence to provide distinction and global recognition to my design. Furthermore, Google's commitment to sustainability and AI reflects my vision for a future in which racing embraces environmentally friendly strategies and intelligent technology. (Sponsor Ref.)
